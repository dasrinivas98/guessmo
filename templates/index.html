🎯 GuessMo Rules

Objective: Guess the hidden 4-letter word in 8 tries or less.

🕹️ How to Play

  • Enter a 4-letter English word where all letters are unique (no repeats like MOON ❌).
  • Press Enter or click Submit to check your guess.
  • Each letter tile changes color based on feedback:
    • 🟩 Green — Letter is correct and in the right position.
    • 🟨 Yellow — Letter is in the word but in the wrong position.
    • ⬜ Gray — Letter is not in the word.
  • Use the feedback to form your next guess.
  • You have 8 chances to find the correct word.

📅 Daily Word

The secret word changes every day. Once you’ve guessed today’s word (or used all 8 tries), you can come back tomorrow for a new one!

🚫 Rules

  • Only valid English words are accepted.
  • Duplicate letters are not allowed in your guess.
  • After 8 incorrect attempts, the correct answer will be revealed.

🏆 Winning

Guess the secret word before you run out of tries to win! Challenge your friends and compare how many attempts it took you.
